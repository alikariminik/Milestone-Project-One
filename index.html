Welcome to Grizzo's

Thank you for visiting our brand new site! We hope you like what you see and we cannot wait to hopefully see you in person very soon.

Grizzo's has been regarded as one of the best reviewed and well regarded restaurants in London ever since it's opening back in 1984. As our reputation grew into becoming one of the foremost eateries in the city, Grizzo's began to welcome celebrities, sports stars and world renowned food-critics through our doors.

Over the last 38 years, we have listened to our loyal customers about what we can do better in order to continiously improve our food, service and overall Grizzo's experience. We have learned a lot, adapted to the changing times, and promise to continue to do so for as long as we turn on our fryers and heat up our grils - that's the Grizzo's promise.
